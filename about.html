Roles

  1. Editors:
    Review articles for appropriateness, clarity, grammar, and plagiarism as well as lead and coordinate members of a group.
  2. Columnists:
    Write consistent articles to be featured in the Churchill Chronicle on topics ranging from food to self-help.
  3. Reporters:
    Report on current or past school or community events, and interview members of the community.
  4. Writers:
    Write freely with no set structure from article to article, includes poetry, prose, short stories and more.
